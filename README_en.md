# Problematic Smartphone Use and Mental Health in Adolescents in Latin America (2015–2024)

Database on problematic smartphone use and its impact on the mental health of adolescents in Latin America (2015-2024). Includes prevalence by country, age group, gender, type of use, and mental health indicators.
